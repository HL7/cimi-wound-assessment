Profile Definition
Wound Assertion An assertion of presence of a wound. The profile contains all of the needed, qualifying data elements (as extensions) to fully document a wound. This inlcudes a highly structured body location as well as a panel of observations that describe the wound such as Wound Size, Wound Tunelling, Wound Underming, Color descriptions, and more.
Wound Related Observations Panel A panel of observations that aid in describing and assessing a wound.
Wound Bed A description of the bed of the wound; its appearance, color, and edge.
Wound Bed Appearance A coded description of the wound bed.
Wound Bed Color A description of the color or colors of a wound bed. Along with a percentage of the total wound bed for each observed color.
Wound Edge A grouping of observations of a wound edge, its color and appearance.
Wound Edge Color A description of the color or colors of a wound edge, inlcuding the color's percentage of the whole wound edge.
Wound Edge Description A coded description of the wound edge appearance.
Wound Exudate Assert An assertion of the presence of the discharge of fluid from a wound, inlcuding a relative amount, exudate color, exudate odor, and a measurement of the volume of discharge.
Wound Exudate Amount Description A coded description of the realative amount of exudate such as "copious", "moderate", or "small".
Wound Exudate Appearance A coded description of the exudate appearance.
Wound Exudate Color A coded description of the exudate color.
Wound Exudate Odor A coded description of the exudate odor.
Wound Exudate Volume A physical quantity measurement of the volume of fluid discharge.
Wound Shape A coded description of the general shape of a wound.
Wound Size A set of measurements of the wound; length, width, depth, area, volume, and qualitative wound area.
Wound Tunneling Assert An assertion of the presence wound tunneling, inlcuding its direction and length.
Wound Tunneling Direction The direction that a specific wound tunnel travels away from the center of a wound. Given as a clock face position; 12 o'clock, 1 o'clock, etc.
Wound Tunneling Length The measured length of a wound tunnel.
Wound Undermining Assert The assertion of the presence of wound undermining.
Wound Undermining Direction The direction that the wound undermining travels away from the center of the wound. Given as a clock face position; 12 o'clock, 1 o'clock, etc.
Wound Undermining Length The measured length of the wound undermining.
NPUAP Wound Grade The National Pressure Ulcer Advisory Panel pressure injury grading system
PEDIS Wound Grade Total Score The PEDIS (perfusion, extent, depth, infection, and sensitivity) diabetic foot ulcer total scoring system.
SINBAD Wound Grade Total Score The SINBAD (site, ischemia, neuropathy, bacterial infection, and depth) diabetic foot ulcer total scoring system.
University of Texas Wound Grade The University of Texas diabetic foot ulcer grading system.
Wagner Wound Grade The Wagner diabetic foot ulcer grading system.
